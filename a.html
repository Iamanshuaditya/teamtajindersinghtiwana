Madad Ka thikana, Tajinder Singh Tiwana

Empowering Citizens Through Leadership and Engagement

Madad Ka Thikana offers hands-on assistance with a wide range of public services to simplify the process for citizens. The initiative helps with voter registration, E-Shram card, PAN card, senior citizen card, Aadhaar card, and provides guidance on health schemes such as Ayushman Bharat and the Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana. It also provides accurate information about post office policies, BMC services, and government welfare schemesempowering people to access the support they deserve.
  • Helping citizens with Aadhaar, PAN, E-Shram, and senior citizen card applications.
  • Guiding communities on healthcare schemes and government benefits.
